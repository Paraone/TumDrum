About

Tum Drum is a cross between Simon and Guitar hero.

You are a drummer. It's your time to perform! TumDrum follows all the basic rules of the game Simon with some added bonuses, including, a drum pad istead of some lame beeping buttons, A background song that gives a rhythm to the sequence you follow and animated sprites to show your progression in the game.
